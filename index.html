The Big Idea

Theme Meaning

"Finding North — Navigating Life's Unwritten Map is about discovering your own direction when life doesn't give you a guide. It's a reminder that even without a perfect plan, you can create your own compass, trust it, and move confidently toward your purpose."
  • Navigating uncertainty and embracing the unknown
  • Exploring passions and personal discovery
  • Redefining success on your own terms
  • Creating your own compass in a world without maps
Why This Matters

Purpose: Why Do We Need It?

01

Real Role Models

Hearing from entrepreneurs and authors who've already "found their spark" shows students what's actually possible. Real stories from real people who navigated their own unwritten maps.

02

Inspiration at the Right Age

High school is when motivation shapes habits, confidence, and future paths. This is the critical window where inspiration can change trajectories.

03

What Textbooks Can't Teach

Passion, risk-taking, purpose, and resilience become real when shared by people who lived it. These are the lessons that don't fit in standard curricula.

04

Shorten the Confusion Phase

Seeing how others found their "North" helps students start discovering their own. Learning from others' journeys accelerates personal growth.
