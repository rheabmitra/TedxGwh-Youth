The Big Idea

Theme Meaning

"Finding North — Navigating Life's Unwritten Map is about discovering your own direction when life doesn't give you a guide. It's a reminder that even without a perfect plan, you can create your own compass, trust it, and move confidently toward your purpose."
  • Navigating uncertainty and embracing the unknown
  • Exploring passions and personal discovery
  • Redefining success on your own terms
  • Creating your own compass in a world without maps
Why This Matters

Why we need this

01

Real stories from innovators

Most of us only see the end result of success. By bringing in entrepreneurs and authors who have already found their spark, we show you the messy middle. It's proof that your "what if" can actually happen.

02

The perfect time for a pivot

High school is where your habits take root. This is the window where a single conversation or a new perspective can shift your entire trajectory. We're here to make sure that shift is toward something you love.

03

The lessons textbooks skip

You can't memorize grit or highlight passion for a test. These are lived experiences. We're diving into the stuff that matters: taking risks, building resilience, and finding a purpose that actually sticks.

04

Stop waiting for a map

The "confusion phase" doesn't have to last forever. Hearing how others navigated their own North helps you stop second-guessing and start moving with confidence.

The Voices

Speakers

Each talk would be 8–20 minutes, in true TED format.

Swami Manohar
Technology Pioneer & Innovator
A pioneer in Indian technology who co-invented the Simputer and co-founded Strand Life Sciences, India's first academic incubating company. PhD from Brown University and legacy as an IISc professor. Recipient of the Dewang Mehta Award and founder of the Jed-i program dedicated to nurturing the next generation of "gifted" engineers.
Vittal Bhandary
Visionary Educationist
Scaled Little Elly from a single center to a massive network of 140+ preschools and K-12 institutions. A master of sustainable scalability, successfully bridging the gap between child-centric learning and corporate childcare for giants like Wipro and TVS Motors. Accomplished speaker at forums like the Education Innovation Summit.
Pankaj Chhaparwal
Founder, Cricbuzz
Transformed a 2004 startup into a global digital powerhouse with over 125 million monthly active users. Master of niche disruption, successfully steered the brand through acquisition by Times Internet while maintaining its status as the world's most-visited cricket destination.
Chirantan Bhabhra
Founder, Storywallahs & Catalyst
Founder of Storywallahs and Catalyst Educational Initiatives, with experience building ventures at the intersection of education, communication, storytelling, and leadership training. Expert in narrative-driven learning and educational innovation.
